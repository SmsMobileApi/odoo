📱 SMS Mobile API for Odoo

Send SMS directly from Odoo using your own mobile phone and the SMSMobileAPI app.

✅ Key Features:

  • Send SMS from contacts and invoices
  • SMS sent using your personal mobile phone
  • Replies go directly to your phone
  • No external gateways — no extra SMS cost

🚀 How it works:

This module connects to the SMSMobileAPI app installed on your smartphone. SMS are sent through your mobile plan, so:

  • You don’t pay any extra SMS fees
  • Recipients see your own phone number
  • You receive replies directly on your mobile

📝 Requirements:

  • A free account on smsmobileapi.com
  • The SMSMobileAPI mobile app installed on your phone (Android or iOS)

Each account comes with a 3-day free trial. No credit card required.
